Here’s a little bit about us…

What we do

EMDB organise and promote one-off concerts both at traditional music venues, and outdoor events /festivals at non-traditional outdoor venues, such as county showgrounds, racecourses, cricket grounds and stadiums.

Who are we

Run by Joshua Stevens a successful entrepreneur who’s non-conventional thinking and style has shaken up several business sectors

Our mission

EMDB mission is to bring you high profile artists at fun, unorthodox, exciting one-off events outside of their traditional album/tour cycle.

Why we do it

EMDB also have a strong community ethos, profit sharing with both local and national charities as well as working closely with Sussex University giving opportunities for students.
